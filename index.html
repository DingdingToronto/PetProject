24 Points Game
RULE OF THE GAME: In the game of 24 points, you're given four numbers, like 4, 6, 8, and 2, and you have to use addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division to make the number 24. You can only use each number once, and you have to end up with a whole number. For example, with 4, 6, 8, and 2, you could say 6 divided by (8 divided by 2) times 4. It's like a puzzle where you get to play with numbers and try to make them add up to 24 in different ways.it's a fun way to practice math!
